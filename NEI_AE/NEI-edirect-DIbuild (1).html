Impact investing enables your clients to achieve their financial goals while making measurable improvements to society and the environment. In our view, it’s the highest form of responsible investing.

NEI is proud to be the first asset manager in Canada to offer a full suite of global impact solutions. With funds in the fixed income, balanced, and equity categories, we give you the tools to align your clients’ financial goals with their values.

NEI Global Impact Initiative is a 3-hour virtual event held over 2 days, where we bring together some of our top sub-advisors to discuss their investment processes, highlight the latest trends in impact investing, and share their success stories.

NEI Global Sustainable Balanced Fund (formerly NEI Balanced RS Fund) has a 60/40 equity/fixed income split that delivers measurable improvement to the environment across both asset classes
