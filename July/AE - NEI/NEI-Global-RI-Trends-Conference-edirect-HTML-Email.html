Responsible investing is everywhere these days. Asset managers across the country are stating their case and promoting their expertise. But if you’re in the business of building actively managed, globally diversified RI portfolios, where do you turn for a credible viewpoint that reaches beyond Canada?

With a roster of 17 hand-picked, unaffiliated sub-advisors from all over the world, NEI offers the kind of boots-on-the-ground perspective that’s incredibly hard to beat. Our sub-advisors serve the needs of institutional and retail investors globally. Every day they are meeting the regional challenges of ESG integration, data analysis, security valuation and selection, impact reporting, and corporate engagement and policy work. Together, we’re able to provide insight into opportunities and risks that you can use to build and monitor better RI portfolios for clients.

NEI Global RI Trends Conference is a premier, two-and-a-half-hour virtual event that brings together 5 of NEI’s top sub-advisors with operations in Canada, the U.S., Europe and Asia. We’ll be focusing on 3 key areas.
The global landscape
An overview of regional trends across asset classes, with an update on fast-moving regulatory developments likely to affect Canadian investors.

How data makes the difference
Speakers will identify the challenges of obtaining reliable and relevant data in various regions and explore the opportunities open to those who get it right.

Engaging for change
Corporate engagement is a key differentiator among leading RI asset managers, but the relationship to investment returns is not always clear. Our speakers will make the connection.
 
We look forward to your participation.
