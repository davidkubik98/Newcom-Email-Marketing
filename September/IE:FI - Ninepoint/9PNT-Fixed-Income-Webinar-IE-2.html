Now available:
Ninepoint Alternative Credit Opportunities Fund

Available to every Canadian investor who seeks additional portfolio income and diversification, Ninepoint introduces a new liquid alternative fund: the Ninepoint Alternative Credit Opportunities Fund.

WHY WE LIKE THIS STRATEGY

Modeled on a proven alternative strategy previously available only to high-net-worth investors, this fund offers:

  • More opportunities for income: Strategy includes use of hybrid securities (e.g. preferred shares, convertible bonds, structured notes) and ability to apply leverage to investment-grade bonds
  • More flexibility to generate returns: Latitude in security selection enables inclusion of bespoke securities, as well as trading strategies such as interest carry, active trading, yield curve, sector positioning, and short selling
  • Active risk management: Use of hedging tools to protect investors from interest rate risk and credit risk
  • Easy access for all Canadian investors as a liquid alternative investment
