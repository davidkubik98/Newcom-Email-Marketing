Invesco discusses expanded opportunities
for accessing innovation on the Nasdaq
 

 
 

Invesco’s Lisa-Marie McDermott, Head of Wealth Management Platforms is joined by Ryan McCormick, Factor and Core Equity Strategist, Invesco and Nasdaq's Mark Marex, Research & Development Specialist, Nasdaq Global Information Services, for a discussionon how to build an innovative core portfolio by accessing the world’s most innovation companies for today and beyond.

Invesco recently expanded the innovation suite that offers a robust line-up of funds and ETFs that provide access to the Nasdaq 100 Index, Nasdaq Next Generation 100 Index and Nasdaq 100 Equal Weighted Index. The webinar will discuss recent highlight saround these indices, portfolio construction ideas and the benefits of the Invesco products.
