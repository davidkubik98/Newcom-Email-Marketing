COP26 Conference—Outcomes, Agreements, and Investment Implications
 
With the UN Climate Change Conference (COP26) now concluded, we evaluate the key outcomes and agreements as international representatives sought to limit the pace and implications of climate change. In a recent video, T. Rowe Price Director of Responsible Investing Maria Elena Drew shares insight on the:
  • Main objectives of the 2021 COP26 Conference
  • Key outcomes from the negotiations and specific agreements reached
  • Impacts these commitments ultimately may have on companies and industries around the world
  • Implications for ESG research
