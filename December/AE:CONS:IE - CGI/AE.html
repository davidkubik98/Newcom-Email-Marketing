Technology continues to rapidly evolve and, with it, investor expectations are shifting. The industry is undergoing large-scale transformations focused on enabling greater capacity and holistic advice delivery.

Prioritizing technologies that will make advisors' lives simpler and their day-to-day activities easier, is critical to future success. CGI is surveying Canadian financial advisors across a variety of segments, intent on distilling a holistic view on how technology is impacting the ways in which advisors interact with their clients and ultimately do business.

We're inviting advisors across Canada to participate in CGI’s Voice of the Advisor research initiative. We hope to gain actionable insights and distill that information for you and your teams to drive better business outcomes.

To thank you for your participation, you'll receive early access to advisor insights which includes:
  • Best practices to driving deeper client engagement
  • The most successful ways to nurture long-term client relationships
  • A chance to win one of eight(8) Apple Watches
