How do we get from awareness to action on risk?

It's a complex problem. Awareness is a precondition for action, but - as we've seen time and again - just knowing about a risk doesn't mean the one at risk will take steps to protect themselves.

Is this 'The Ostrich Paradox' in action? Are risk communicators falling short? How do you inspire collaboration between stakeholders with different perspectives and values, even when they share a goal?

These questions are at the heart of CatIQ Connect's next quarterly webinar, Communicating and Collaborating to Mitigate Loss Due to Catastrophes and Climate Change, taking place on June 10, 2021.
