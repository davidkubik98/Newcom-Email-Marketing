Not all ETFs are created equal.

TD Asset Management is expanding its ETF offering with our new TD Global Healthcare Leaders Index ETF (TDOC). With this future-focused product, you can help your clients tap into a growing sector that may deliver strong returns by gaining exposure to global healthcare companies, not just those in the U.S. TDOC offers access to a globally diversified healthcare index exclusive to TD.
