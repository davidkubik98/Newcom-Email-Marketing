COVID-19 is de-railing Canadians’ retirement plans. You can help get them back on track.
 
We’ve studied how Canadians approaching retirement are preparing and feeling about the next step and have found that the closer they get to retiring, the less positive they feel about it. In fact, some Canadians are thinking about delaying their retirement due to COVID-19.1
With the value of your advice and Canada Life investment solutions, you can help your near-retiree clients with new and different ways of thinking about their investments. Together, you can build a retirement plan they feel confident in.
