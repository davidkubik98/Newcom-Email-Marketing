Buying or Selling Your Book?
Succession Planning Lessons from Both Sides

You have choices in how to succeed your business when the time comes: sell to a peer, succeed to a family member, or exit after selling to a strategic buyer. But how will you determine what’s right for you and your business? Or what’s right for your staff and the clients you’ve served all these years? You learn from those who’ve travelled ahead.

Join us for a highly engaging, interactive discussion and hear from entrepreneurial advisors who have walked different paths and made choices as sellers, buyers, and successors. Hear stories and gain valuable insights that can help you make the right choices for your own succession plan and avoid the pitfalls.
