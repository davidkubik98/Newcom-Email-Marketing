Addenda Capital launches two new climate transition funds
Addenda Capital is proud to take the next step in supporting the climate transition. We are pleased to introduce our two new investment funds designed to encourage public companies that are making true and tangible efforts to reduce greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions in pursuit of a resilient, netzero emissions society by 2050 â€” while also generating compelling financial returns.

Among the first of their kind in the Canadian market, these sustainable investment solutions are the Addenda Climate Transition Canadian Equity Pooled Fund and the Addenda Climate Transition International Equity Pooled Fund.

Building on a solid track record of engagement with companies on climate change, we believe this path is a constructive, inclusive approach to drive the shift for a lower carbon economy.
How will our approach make a difference?
  • Companies are chosen based on clear steps they intend to take to reduce their emissions
  • Sustained engagement with management helps companies in achieving specific goals.
  • Selection criteria and carbon footprint limit will be strengthened in phases over the years.
These robust strategies meet the need for investing in cleaner and impact solutions to spur the climate transition we need. Join us in making the right choice for a better future.
