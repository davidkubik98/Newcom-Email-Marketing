Operational Responsibilities
for Racking System Planning,
Implementation, and Maintenance
WED. FEBRUARY 23, 1:00 PM ET

In this webinar, attendees will learn:
  • What to look for when selecting a racking partner during the design and permitting of a new or existing warehouse space
  • The responsibilities of a warehouse operator with respect to OHS requirements, Racking PM Inspection programs, Repairs, and Required Signage
  • The value of Domestic supply partners over the service lifespan of the Racking system
Attendees will takeaway key information that will help to ensure all of their bases are covered when it comes to implementing one of the most important aspects of their business, their storage system. This is crucial knowledge that will assist in running a successful warehouse/storage operation.
