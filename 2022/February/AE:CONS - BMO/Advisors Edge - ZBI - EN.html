Introducing BMO Canadian Bank Income Index ETF (Ticker: ZBI): Innovation in Fixed Income
Innovation in Fixed Income:
Earn higher yields with the strength of Canadian bank credit
Investors looking to generate additional yield and mitigate rising rates now have a new choice: an ETF that provides access to higher-yielding securities, combined with the strength of Canadian bank credit.
BMO Canadian Bank Income Index ETF (ZBI) provides efficient access to bonds, preferred shares and limited recourse capital notes (LRCNs) issued by the Canadian banks, all in one ETF.
