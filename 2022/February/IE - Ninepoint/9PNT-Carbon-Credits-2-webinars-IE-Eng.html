Carbon Credits promise to be a big part of our future:

  • COP26 endorsed Carbon Credits as a key transitional strategy to a carbon-neutral future.
  • Carbon trading markets already have a combined asset value of over $851 billion and are expected to grow as much as 2500% over the next 30 years.*
  • McKinsey & Company has commented: "Our analysis shows that if investors allocated even a small part of their portfolios to carbon allowances, they could improve the resilience of their portfolios against climate transition risks".**
