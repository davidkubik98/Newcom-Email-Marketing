Introducing Ninepoint Energy Income Fund

 

Energy continues to be a vital sector as the world undertakes a multi-decade transition to a lower carbon future. Join us to hear from Eric Nuttall, Partner and Senior Portfolio Manager, Ninepoint Energy Fund, as he discusses the oil side of this generational energy transition opportunity and his strategy to navigate it via the Ninepoint Energy Income Fund – offering investors capital appreciation and consistent income by investing in dividend paying energy companies.

Some of the topics that Eric will be discussing include:

  • The outlook for dividend paying energy companies
  • Why sector valuations remain extremely compelling with valuations still at or near all-time lows
  • Why he believes demand for oil and gas will continue to soar against the background of high-profile energy transition programs
