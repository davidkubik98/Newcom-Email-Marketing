2022 ETF Portfolio Positioning: Q1 BMO ETF Strategy Report

Alfred Lee, Director, BMO ETFs, and Portfolio Manager & Investment Strategist, BMO Asset Management Inc.
Alfred Lee, Director, BMO ETFs, and Portfolio Manager & Investment Strategist, BMO Asset Management Inc., provides the latest strategy and insights for optimal portfolio positioning in the current market environment.

Expectations for 2022:
  • Tightening monetary policy, persistent inflation and ongoing higher rates, the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, developments in China, and the U.S. mid-terms are the main risks faced by equities this year.
  • Fed tapering and steepening yield curves will cause investors to rotate out of high-growth areas and into more defensive-oriented stocks.
  • Equities will present better risk/reward than bonds, but with more moderated returns, compared to 2021.
  • The prospect of COVID being declared endemic would cause the economy to thrive, putting upward pressure on rates.
