Your partner in continuing education
 

Behind on your continuing education (CE) credits? Deadline looming for insurance CE? Turn procrastination into progress with a visit to CE Corner, where Canada’s financial and insurance professionals learn and earn — earn CE credits, that is.

Visit CE Corner now to take up to four free courses on trending topics such as ESG. In the webinar Desmystifying ESG: Does ESG investing drive better performance?, J.P. Morgan Asset Management peels back the layers and dives deep into what drives the financial performance of ESG investments.

Take this course and receive one CE credit (ICM, ICS, FP Canada, The Institute).

To receive unlimited access to courses, become a member for $199.* Members receive exclusive benefits:

  • Access to Import Certificate, a tool to import credits earned outside CE Corner
  • Members-only newsletter, a monthly wrap-up of available courses, CE deadlines, industry news and compliance tips
