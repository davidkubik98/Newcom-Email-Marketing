Your partner in continuing education
 

Behind on your continuing education (CE) credits? Deadline looming for insurance CE? Turn procrastination into progress with a visit to CE Corner, where financial pros learn and earn — earn CE credits, that is.

Plus, get insight into topics that will help you serve clients better — such as ESG. ESG products represent one-third of global assets under management, so acquiring a greater understanding of these products is increasingly necessary. In the webinar Desmystifying ESG: Does ESG investing drive better performance?, J.P. Morgan Asset Management peels back the layers and dives deep into what drives the financial performance of ESG investments.

Take this course and receive one CE credit (ICM, ICS, FP Canada, The Institute).
