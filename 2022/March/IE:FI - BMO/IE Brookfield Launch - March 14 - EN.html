The strength of two great asset managers

With market volatility, rising prices and low yields creating many challenges for investors, the search is on for alternative sectors that can boost returns while diversifying the asset mix away from traditional holdings.
BMO Global Asset Management and Brookfield PSG have collaborated to launch new mutual funds and ETF series that combine their respective expertise in investment management and real asset strategy.
BMO Brookfield Global Renewables Infrastructure Fund and ETF Series offer investors access to sustainability infrastructure assets and renewable energy companies, capitalizing on the multi-decade transition from oil and coal to alternative fuel sources.
