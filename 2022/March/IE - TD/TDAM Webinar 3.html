Gain a better understanding of what’s happening in the technology sector, where it’s heading and why you should be paying attention.
 

Accreditation pending for FP Canada, IIROC and The Institute
 
 

It's no secret that technology stocks have skyrocketed over the last couple of years, and while many have benefited from the rise, the results over the last quarter have a growing chorus of investors believing the next year may have its bumps.

Despite, the current market landscape presenting some challenges for the technology sector, it continues to be a space that offers many opportunities.

Join us for a deep dive into what's happening in the technology sector, where we think it's heading and why it's important to understand what you're getting when it comes to technology exposure.
