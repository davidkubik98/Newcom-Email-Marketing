The trajectory of technology adoption has accelerated. Insurers are investing in digital technologies that will improve their customers’ experiences, promote innovation and reduce costs.

In this webcast, OneSpan will be joined by Guest Speaker Oliwia Berdak, VP and Research Director at Forrester Research, in a discussion around how insurers can benchmark their digital functionalities against peers, assess their digital insurance maturity, and evaluate digital transformation competencies.


Topics include:

  • How digital tech is changing the industry, including key enabling technologies
  • How to accelerate transformation with third-party providers and core systems
  • The role of e-signature and digital identity verification in completing digital processes
