Introducing new Invesco ETFs that access the innovators of the Nasdaq
 
 
 
 
 

Today, we’re thrilled to launch new exchange-traded funds that provide access to the world’s most innovative companies. These ETFs complement QQC.F, our Innovation Suite’s flagship ETF, and the suite’s line-up of mutual funds that we launched in January of this year. At Invesco, we are continuing to offer new ways to invest in the innovators of Nasdaq.

By expanding the Invesco Innovation Suite, we’re again leading the way in connecting Canadian investors to innovators in the Nasdaq and making it easier for you to find the right investment solution for each client. When the QQQ strategy launched in the US 20 years ago, it was a pioneer in simplifying how investors gained access to companies within the Nasdaq 100 Index. Now, Canadians have even more ways to access the Nasdaq 100 Index and Nasdaq Next Gen 100 Index with Invesco.
