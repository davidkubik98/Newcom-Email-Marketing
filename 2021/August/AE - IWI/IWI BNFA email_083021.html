Behavioral Negotiation for Advisors

October 4, 2021

 

LIVE Zoom Workshop - Space is Limited!

 

Join other top advisors at the Behavioral Negotiation for Advisors 2021 conference! This event will be conducted virtually in a Zoom workshop format with Daylian Cain, PhD Senior Lecturer in Negotiations, Leadership and Ethics, Yale School of Management.

 

This 4.5-hour workshop will focus on the behavioral approach to negotiation.

 

Presentation Topic: Negotiation Mind Games - Collaborating with Difficult People

 

Learning Objectives:

  • Demonstrate how to create value for all parties through collaboration.
  • Discover how to “sell” your idea.
  • Analyze saving time & money when allocating resources across constituents.
  • Develop practical negotiation skills as you explore common mistakes.
  • Explore question-asking techniques that allow you to “get into the other side’s head.”

 

Agenda: The workshop is 4.5 hours, including a Zoom breakout session for a negotiation exercise, several breaks (approx. every hour), and Q&A.
