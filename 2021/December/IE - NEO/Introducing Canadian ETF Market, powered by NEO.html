The Canadian ETF industry is booming, yet access to real-time professional data is limited. Until now. Introducing the Canadian ETF Market, powered by NEO.

The Canadian ETF industry is booming as more investors and advisors become aware of their benefits. Today, Canadian investors can choose among more than 1,200 ETFs listed in Canada, yet access to real-time institutional-grade data to make an informed decision is limited, at best.

Until now. 
 
Introducing the Canadian ETF Market – a centralized hub providing ETF research and insights for investors and advisors that need timely and holistic data to compare, contrast, and select Canadian ETFs.
 
Brought to you by the NEO Exchange and ETF specialist Trackinsight, ETF Market is the first platform of its kind to offer all-in access to the entire universe of Canadian-listed ETFs, without charge, for all users. 
 
Check out www.ETFMarket.ca where you’ll find:

  • Real-time market data and analytics on all Canadian-listed ETFs, available in both English and French
  • NAV-based performance returns
  • Transparent holdings information
  • Sophisticated digital screeners and selection tools
  • Proprietary research, ETF news and commentary
  • Investor education resources on essential topics such as asset allocation, risk management, ESG investing, and portfolio construction
  • Intelligent search functionality, and so much more.
