As remote work has become the norm for many Canadian organizations adopting an “anywhere” approach to conducting business, e-signatures have emerged as a perfect fit in these digital shift plans.

Whether or not your company is already leveraging e-signatures, now is the time to build for the future and get an in-depth look at different e-signature use cases.


In this webinar, OneSpan’s digital experts will cover:

  • Walkthrough and technical steps in the e-signature implementation process
  • B2E, B2B and B2C workflows
  • Tips for winning internal adoption
  • How to replicate in-person interactions through digital platforms that integrate e-signature, videoconferencing, identity proofing, and rich collaboration capabilities
