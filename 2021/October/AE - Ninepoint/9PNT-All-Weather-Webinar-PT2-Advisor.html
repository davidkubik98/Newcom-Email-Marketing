How to Build an All-Weather Portfolio:
Real Asset Edition

It is widely accepted that portfolio diversification is desirable.

The big question is how to best achieve that goal in a way that reduces overall portfolio volatility.

In the next edition of this 4-part webinar series, Jeff Sayer, Portfolio Manager on multiple Ninepoint real asset funds, will consider the role of real estate and infrastructure as hedges against inflation and as reliable sources of income – all in pursuit of building a portfolio that is better designed to withstand the uncertainties of the market.

Jeffrey Sayer is Vice President, Portfolio Manager at Ninepoint Partners. Jeff oversees Global Equity, US Equity, Infrastructure, and Real Estate investment strategies for the firm. Prior to Ninepoint’s formation, Jeff was with Sprott Asset Management (SAM), rounding out more than 17 years of investment management experience. Before joining SAM, Jeff held the position of Portfolio Manager at a Toronto-based investment counselor, focused primarily on separately managed accounts. He has held previous positions of Associate Portfolio Manager and Equity Research Analyst at I.A. Michael Investment Counsel, manager of the ABC Funds.
