The growth of ESG – and what it means for investors
Do you understand the investment impact of an ESG focus?
Considerations of environmental, social and governance (ESG) factors have become a dominant force in the investment industry. There are now 84 ESG-focused ETFs in Canada, 34 of which launched in 2021 alone. Sustainability and positive returns aren’t mutually exclusive. In fact, performance in both is connected.
