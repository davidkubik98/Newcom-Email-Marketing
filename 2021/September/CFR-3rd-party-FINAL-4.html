Are Firms Ready for Client Focused Reforms? 
The Client Focused Reforms (CFRs) will take effect in Canada at the end of 2021. Advisors and the firms that they represent must enhance processes and technology to meet the new rules. Morningstar conducted a survey to better understand attitudes toward the regulation, preparedness for compliance, and the impact to internal processes to try and determine—are firms ready for CFR? 

Who We Surveyed 
We surveyed over 200 advisor and head office professionals representing both bank-owned and independent dealer networks to get a representative sample of opinions towards the CFRs.
