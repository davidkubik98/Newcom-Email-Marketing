Sustainable investing has become mainstream but approaches still vary substantially, according to the results of a new study of global institutional investors commissioned by AGF and conducted by Coalition Greenwich.
At our upcoming event, join experts from AGF and Coalition Greenwich who will discuss the survey findings and explore different approaches to integrating sustainable investments into portfolios.
Event Sessions
• Targeting Impact: Dive into the Coalition Greenwich Global Institutional Investor Survey Results
A deep dive into the shift towards full ESG integration, use of thematic sustainable strategies and how asset owners are measuring the impact of their sustainable investments.
• Exploring a Thematic Approach to Sustainable Investing
How allocations to thematic investment strategies have the potential to enhance impact levels, diversify portfolios and provide an additional source of alpha generation to investors.
• ESG and the Mainstream: How Local Regulatory Changes are Influencing Global Asset Manager Behaviour
A look at the changing regulatory landscape for sustainable investments and what this means for portfolio managers who are incorporating measurable environmental and social impact alongside traditional performance metrics.
