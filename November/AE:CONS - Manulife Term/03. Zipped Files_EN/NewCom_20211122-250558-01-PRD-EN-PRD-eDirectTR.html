Manulife has recently lowered Term‑10 and Term‑20 rates1 for Family Term, Business Term and Family Term with  Vitality. This means your clients can get the term protection they need at a price they can afford.
So, how much have rates decreased?
• Up to 13% for T‑10 policies2
• Up to 10% for T‑20 policies2
Manulife term products offer protection for a range of client needs, including family protection and business continuity.

Plus, there’s even more to get excited about! No fluid testing limits have been increased for life insurance applicants age 18‑50, for insurance amounts up to and including $5,000,000. Now more of your clients can apply for life insurance without a visit from a health professional and enjoy an easier, less intrusive experience, with faster decisions.
