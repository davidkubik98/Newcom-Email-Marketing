No longer the “factory to the world,” China is now a leader in innovation and entrepreneurship. With a focus on domestic growth, this fully developed economy deserves a standalone allocation in any growth-focused portfolio.

Mackenzie has access to the Chinese market, with boots on the ground to provide perspective that you won’t get elsewhere.

On June 22, our distinguished guest speakers will join our Mackenzie China experts to paint a picture of how China is shifting from export reliance toward more sustainable growth driven by technology and consumer sectors. They’ll also provide unique insights on its capital market structure.

June 22 at 1 pm ET
China: Emerging no longer
Fresh perspectives from experts on the ground

REGISTER NOW
