What is My Plan and how can I use it?

My Plan allows you to manage and track your credits by creating a customized plan as per your designation requirements. Create a plan based on the number of credits and competencies required for the term. As you successfully complete a course, My Plan will update automatically to reflect credits earned from the completed course. (You must be logged into CE Corner to access My Plan).

Unlock Tax-Free Cash From Whole Insurance
